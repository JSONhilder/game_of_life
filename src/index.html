Conway's Game of Life

Game of Life (or just “Life”) is not really a game. There’s no winning or losing or destroying your opponent mentally and spiritually.

Life is a “cellular automaton” - a system of cells that live on a grid, where they live, die and evolve according to the rules that govern their world.

Game Rules

Each iteration the cells update according to 4 rules:

  • Any live cell with 0 or 1 live neighbors becomes dead, because of underpopulation
  • Any live cell with 2 or 3 live neighbors stays alive, because its neighborhood is just right
  • Any live cell with more than 3 live neighbors becomes dead, because of overpopulation
  • Any dead cell with exactly 3 live neighbors becomes alive, by reproduction
